Our Story & Mission

TGC Titans Gaming Community was forged in 2024 from a shared dream: to build an inclusive, exhilarating, and supportive haven for gamers of all stripes. Whether you're a casual enthusiast looking for friends or a competitive player aiming for the top, we believe in the power of gaming to connect, inspire, and create unforgettable experiences.

Our core mission is to cultivate a positive and dynamic environment where sportsmanship, teamwork, and mutual respect flourish. We strive to offer diverse gaming opportunities, host engaging events, and provide a platform for members to grow, share their passion, and be part of something truly special.

TGC Titans: Official Rules

Welcome to the TGC Titans Gaming Community! To ensure a fun, fair, and respectful environment for all members, please read and adhere to the following rules. Violation of these rules may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or a permanent ban from the community, depending on the severity and frequency of the offense.

Section 1: General Conduct & Respect

  • Respect All Members: Treat everyone with respect, regardless of their skill level, background, age, gender, race, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, or any other personal attribute. Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, and bullying in any form will not be tolerated.
  • No Toxicity: This includes, but is not limited to, flaming, excessive negativity, personal attacks, insults, or any behavior intended to provoke or upset other members. Keep it positive!
  • Keep it Clean: Avoid excessive profanity. While some mature language might be acceptable in certain contexts (depending on game ratings and specific channel rules), overly offensive or vulgar language is prohibited. No NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content outside of designated channels (if any).
  • No Doxxing or Sharing Personal Information: Do not share personal information about yourself or others without explicit consent. This includes real names, addresses, phone numbers, social media profiles, etc.
  • Impersonation: Do not impersonate other members, moderators, administrators, or any other individual.
  • Follow Instructions: Adhere to the instructions and decisions of TGC Admin and Moderator staff. If you have a concern about a decision, please address it privately and respectfully with a staff member.

Section 2: Communication & Channel Usage

  • Use Appropriate Channels: Post content in the relevant channels. Off-topic discussions should be kept to designated off-topic or general chat areas.
  • No Spamming: Avoid spamming chat channels with repetitive messages, excessive emojis, large blocks of text, or unsolicited advertisements.
  • Voice Chat Etiquette:
    • Be mindful of background noise. Use push-to-talk if necessary.
    • Do not intentionally disrupt voice channels with loud noises, music, or voice changers unless appropriate for the context and agreed upon by participants.
    • Avoid talking over others excessively. Give everyone a chance to speak.
  • Language: The primary language for general community channels is English. If specific language channels are created, please use them accordingly.
  • Advertising & Self-Promotion:
    • Unsolicited advertising of other communities, services, or products is generally not allowed.
    • Some self-promotion (e.g., streaming links, content creation) may be permitted in designated areas or with prior approval from Admin. Check specific channel rules.

Section 3: Gameplay & Fair Play

  • No Cheating or Hacking: The use of cheats, hacks, exploits, or any third-party software that provides an unfair advantage in any game is strictly prohibited.
  • Sportsmanship: Play fair and be a good sport, whether you win or lose. Congratulate opponents and be gracious in defeat.
  • Team Play: When participating in team-based games, cooperate with your teammates and contribute to the team's objectives. Griefing, intentionally sabotaging your team, or refusing to play your role is discouraged.
  • Respect Game-Specific Rules: Adhere to the terms of service and rules of conduct for any game you are playing within the community.
  • Stream Sniping/Ghosting: Do not engage in stream sniping (using a streamer's live broadcast to gain an unfair advantage) or ghosting (providing live information to players in a game they shouldn't have).

Section 4: Community Engagement & Content

  • Constructive Feedback: We welcome constructive feedback about the community. Please share your thoughts in a respectful manner through appropriate channels or directly with staff.
  • Event Participation: We encourage participation in community events, tournaments, and activities. Follow event-specific rules and instructions.
  • Content Sharing: When sharing content (memes, videos, articles), ensure it is appropriate for the community and does not violate any other rules (e.g., NSFW, hate speech). Give credit to original creators where applicable.
  • Spoilers: Be mindful of spoilers for games, movies, or shows. Use spoiler tags or warnings, especially in general channels.

Section 5: Moderation & Rule Enforcement

  • Reporting Violations: If you witness a member violating community rules, please report it to an Admin or Moderator with any relevant evidence (screenshots, VOD clips, etc.). Do not engage in public call-outs; let the staff handle the situation.
  • Disciplinary Actions: Rule violations will be addressed by TGC Admin and Moderator staff. Actions may include:
    • Verbal/written warning
    • Temporary mute or restriction from specific channels
    • Temporary suspension from the community
    • Permanent ban from the community
  • Appeals: If you believe a disciplinary action taken against you was unfair, you may appeal the decision by contacting an Admin privately and respectfully.
  • Rule Updates: These rules are subject to change. Major updates will be announced to the community. It is your responsibility to stay informed about the current rules.

Section 6: Joining TGC Titans & Representing the Community

  • Application/Joining Process (If Applicable): Follow any specified procedures for joining TGC Titans (e.g., application form, tryouts, invitation).
  • Community Tag/Name: If using a TGC community tag or name in-game or on other platforms, represent the community positively and uphold these rules.
  • Be an Ambassador: As a member of TGC Titans, you are an ambassador for our community. Your actions, both within and outside of official TGC spaces, can reflect on the community as a whole. Strive to be a positive influence in the wider gaming world.

By participating in the TGC Titans Gaming Community, you agree to abide by these rules. Our goal is to create a thriving, enjoyable, and inclusive space for all gamers. Let's build something great together!

TGC Admin & Moderator Team
